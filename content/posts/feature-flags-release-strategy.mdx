Feature flags are not just toggles. They are an operational safety net.

## Effective flag lifecycle

1. create flag with owner
2. ship dark
3. rollout by cohort
4. observe metrics
5. remove flag after full rollout

## Two anti-patterns

- permanent flags nobody cleans up
- flags without monitoring tied to the rollout

## Rollout checklist

- canary users first
- alert thresholds defined
- rollback action documented

Flags are great only if they are temporary and measurable.
